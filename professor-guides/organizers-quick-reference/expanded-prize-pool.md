# Expanded Prize Pool Model (No Participation Packs)

Use this when:
- Each kid contributes 1 pack  
- No participation packs  
- Goal: ~50% of players receive prizes  

---

## Steps

1. Prize pool = number of players  
2. No participation packs  
3. Expand Top Cut to ~50% of players  
4. Apply proportional model  

---

## Recommended Top Cuts

8 players → Top 4  
12 players → Top 8  
16 players → Top 8  
20 players → Top 8  
24 players → Top 12 (optional)  

---

## Why This Works

- Fair  
- Scalable  
- Easy to announce  
- Kids understand it  
- No store cost increase
