# Prize Distribution Models

Organizers may choose any legal model as long as it is:
- Announced before Round 1  
- Consistent within the division  
- Not age‑based  

---

## Method A — Participation + Top Cut

1. Every player receives 1 pack  
2. Remaining packs go to top finishers  

Good for:
- Casual events  
- Younger divisions  
- Store‑friendly experiences  

---

## Method B — Pure Top Cut

1. No participation packs  
2. All packs go to top finishers  

Good for:
- Competitive events  
- Older divisions  
- Larger prize pools  

---

## Method C — Expanded Prize Pool (No Participation Packs)

1. Each kid contributes 1 pack  
2. No participation packs  
3. Top Cut expanded to ~50% of players  
4. All packs go to Top Cut  

Good for:
- Large Junior divisions  
- Budget‑friendly events  
- Fair distribution
