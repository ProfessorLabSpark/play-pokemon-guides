# Prizing Rules

Prize pools are calculated **per division**, not per event.

Divisions:
- Juniors  
- Seniors  
- Masters  

Each division has:
- Its own prize pool  
- Its own standings  
- Its own CP awards  

Unified pods affect pairings only.

---

## Prize Pool Formula

Prize Pool = number of players in division × packs per player

Example:
- 14 Juniors × 2 packs = 28 packs  
- 8 Seniors × 2 packs = 16 packs  
- 2 Masters × 2 packs = 4 packs  

---

## Legal Prize Structures

### Method A — Participation + Top Cut
- Every player gets 1 pack  
- Remaining packs go to top finishers  

### Method B — Pure Top Cut
- All packs go to top finishers  
- No participation packs  

### Method C — Expanded Prize Pool (1 pack per kid, no participation)
- All packs go to Top Cut  
- Top Cut expanded to ~50% of players  

All methods must be:
- Announced before Round 1  
- Consistent within the division  
- Not based on age
