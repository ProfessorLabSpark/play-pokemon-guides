# Prize Pool Calculation

Prize pools are calculated **per division**.

Formula:
Prize Pool = number of players × packs per player

---

## Example Event (24 players, 2 packs each)

Masters (2 players):
2 × 2 = 4 packs

Seniors (8 players):
8 × 2 = 16 packs

Juniors (14 players):
14 × 2 = 28 packs

Total event packs = 48  
But each division distributes its own pool.

---

## Notes

- Prize pools never mix between divisions  
- Prize pools do not change after Round 1  
- Drops do not reduce prize pools
